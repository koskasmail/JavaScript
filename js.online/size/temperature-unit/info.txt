-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
f --> k
1 degree Fahrenheit =
255.927778 kelvin

Let's say you have a temperature of 77°F and you want to convert it to Kelvin.
K = (77 - 32) × 5/9 + 273.15
K = 45 × 5/9 + 273.15
K = 25 + 273.15
K = 298.15

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
f --> c
1 degree Fahrenheit =
-17.2222222 degrees Celsius


Celsius = (Fahrenheit - 32) * 5/9

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

c --> f

1 degree Celsius =
33.8 degrees Fahrenheit


Fahrenheit = (Celsius * 9/5) + 32

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

c --> k

1 degree Celsius =
274.15 kelvin


Kelvin = 25 + 273.15 = 298.15 K

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

k --> c

1 kelvin =
-272.15 degrees Celsius


Celsius = Kelvin - 273.15

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

k --> f

1 kelvin =
-457.87 degrees Fahrenheit


Fahrenheit = (Kelvin - 273.15) × 9/5 + 32


Fahrenheit = (300 - 273.15) × 9/5 + 32
Fahrenheit = (26.85) × 9/5 + 32
Fahrenheit = 48.33 + 32
Fahrenheit = 80.63
